# Multi-Views Axioms

## AX-SKILL-MULTI-VIEWS-001: Compose, Don't Duplicate

Delegate per-topic research to `for-and-against`. Do not reimplement directional queries inside this skill. Composition over duplication.

## AX-SKILL-MULTI-VIEWS-002: Independent Per-Topic

Each topic gets its own independent for-and-against run. No cross-contamination — a bullish view on Topic A must not leak into the bearish case for Topic B.

## AX-SKILL-MULTI-VIEWS-003: Cross-Comparison

After per-topic runs, identify shared catalysts, divergent factors, correlations, and contrarian overlaps across topics. This is the value-add of multi-views — the synthesis that individual runs cannot produce.

## AX-SKILL-MULTI-VIEWS-004: Qualitative Ranking Only

Rank topics ordinally (strongest, weakest, most balanced) with direct reasons. No numeric scores, no fabricated precision. "Copper has a stronger qualitative profile than lithium because..." — not "Copper scores 7.3 vs lithium 6.1."
