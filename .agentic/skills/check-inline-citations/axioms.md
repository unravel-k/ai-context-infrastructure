# Check Inline Citations Axioms

See `.agentic/axioms/skills/check-inline-citations.md` for the canonical axiom definitions.

## AX-SKILL-CHECK-INLINE-CITATIONS-001: Every Factual Claim Needs a Citation

Every factual claim must have an inline citation. No claim gets a free pass — not even "common knowledge" unless it's truly uncontroversial (e.g., "water boils at 100°C").

## AX-SKILL-CHECK-INLINE-CITATIONS-002: Citations at Point of Use

Citations must be at the point of use — the same sentence or immediately after. A bibliography at the end is not a substitute for inline citation. The reader must be able to trace any claim to its source without scanning.

## AX-SKILL-CHECK-INLINE-CITATIONS-003: Flag, Don't Fabricate

Never add citations the text doesn't have. Never guess a source. Only report what exists and what's missing. Adding fake citations is worse than missing citations.
